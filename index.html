Bootcamp 2021: pong game

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of arrays, loops, and randomness. this is a two player game were both players are trying to score in each other areas. in this case player one is hit the ball in players two areas therefore meaning that player one get the point. then the ball resets its postion and then for example player two is hit the ball in players one areas therefore meaning that player two get the point.

Check out the code on Github: https://github.com/cechthereplay/bootcamp_project/
